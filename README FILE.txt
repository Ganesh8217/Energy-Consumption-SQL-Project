# ⚡ Energy Consumption Analysis using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes global **energy production, energy consumption, GDP, population, and emissions data** using SQL.
The goal is to extract meaningful insights about energy usage patterns, economic impact, and sustainability trends.

Through SQL queries, this project explores how energy consumption relates to economic growth and environmental impact across different countries.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze the relationship between **energy production and energy consumption**.
* Determine **per capita energy consumption trends**.
* Compare **GDP growth with emissions and energy usage patterns**.
* Identify countries with **unusual energy profiles** (high production but low consumption or vice versa).
* Use **SQL queries for data extraction, aggregation, and analysis**.

---

## 🗂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains global energy-related information including:

* Energy Production
* Energy Consumption
* GDP
* Population
* Carbon Emissions

These datasets were organized into relational tables and analyzed using SQL queries.

---

## 🧩 Database Schema

The project uses a relational database structure where tables are connected using common attributes such as **country and year**.

Key tables include:

* Energy Production
* Energy Consumption
* GDP Data
* Population Data
* Emissions Data

An **Entity Relationship (ER) diagram** was created to visualize the relationships between these tables.

---

## 🔎 Key Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ General & Comparative Analysis

* Compare energy production and consumption between countries.
* Identify the highest and lowest energy-consuming nations.

### 2️⃣ Trend Analysis

* Analyze how energy consumption changes over time.
* Study long-term trends in energy usage and emissions.

### 3️⃣ Per Capita Analysis

* Calculate **energy consumption per capita**.
* Compare consumption levels with population and GDP.

### 4️⃣ Global Comparisons

* Identify countries with unusual energy profiles.
* Compare economic development with energy usage patterns.

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Countries with **high GDP but low renewable production** have strong potential for sustainable energy investments.
* **Per capita energy consumption strongly correlates with GDP**, but emissions efficiency varies significantly.
* **Emerging economies show rapidly increasing energy consumption**, indicating future energy demand.
* Some nations maintain **stable emissions despite economic growth**, demonstrating effective environmental policies.
